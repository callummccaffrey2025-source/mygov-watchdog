## Legal Basis

We display published factual polling numbers (primary votes, two-party preferred, sample sizes) with full attribution to the polling firm. This is permitted under Australian fair dealing for news reporting and commentary.

We do NOT:
- Republish polling firms' full commentary or analysis
- Present our own numbers as if from a polling firm
- Conduct our own polls
- Alter or adjust the published numbers

We DO:
- Display the pollster name prominently on every poll
- Link to the original publication source URL
- Credit Wikipedia as the aggregation source
- Clearly label our "poll of polls" average as a simple average of published polls
